Half-Semester Grading

In half-semester courses, each quarter is worth 40% of your grade, and the final exam is worth 20%.

How to Use This Calculator

1. Enter your grades for Quarter 1 and Quarter 2.

2. Enter your desired final grade.

3. Click "Calculate" to see what score you need on the final exam.

4. If the calculator shows it's not possible to achieve your desired grade, consider adjusting your goal or speaking with your instructor.
